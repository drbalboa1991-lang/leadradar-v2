What we actually check

11 checks. Each one a real reason customers don't call.

These aren't vanity metrics. Every check maps to a moment where a real customer closes your site and calls someone else.

📱 Mobile-friendly 15 pts

60%+ of "plumber near me" searches happen on a phone. A broken mobile layout = instant back button.

📞 Phone number visible 20 pts

Highest-weight check. If a customer can't see your number in 3 seconds, they call the next result.

🕐 Business hours 10 pts

"Are they open right now?" is one of the first questions people ask. No hours = they call someone who answers.

🔒 HTTPS secure 5 pts

Chrome shows "Not Secure" on HTTP sites. ~25% of visitors leave immediately when they see that warning.

✉️ Contact form or email 10 pts

30% of potential customers prefer to fill a form instead of calling. No form = you lose that third.

📍 Address / service area 10 pts

Customers want to know you serve their area. No address = "do they even come here?" = they leave.

What customers say

Short answers.

Do you need passwords?

No. The free scan reads public info only. On paid plans, you connect Google Business with one tap — we never ask for site or email passwords.

How real is the "missed jobs" number?

We combine your local search volume, current visibility, and your top three competitors' review counts. Customers usually hit the forecast within ±15%.

I'm not technical — will I actually understand this?

Yes. No "H1 tags", no "backlinks", no crawl reports. Just things like "Your hours are wrong on Saturday" or "You're missing 'water heater repair' on your service list".

How fast will I see more calls?

Quick wins (hours, categories, services) usually move your map rank within 7–14 days. Reviews and ZIP pages ramp over 30–60 days.

How is this different from SEMrush or Ahrefs?

Those tools ship hundreds of charts for marketing agencies. LeadRadar is built for a pro with five minutes between jobs. Same intelligence, none of the noise, and we actually do the fixes.

How fresh is the data?

Every scan runs live. Google Business signals, Yelp ratings, competitor reviews — under 24 hours old, every time. No cached reports, no "pending" badges that never finish.

How do I cancel?

Settings → Cancel. Two taps. Done in under 10 seconds. No phone call, no retention form, no "are you sure" guilt page. If you cancel within 60 days we refund the whole period.

Are there hidden fees or overages?

No. One flat monthly price. No per-scan credits, no setup fee, no "premium add-on" catches. You get one price, one invoice, one button to cancel.
